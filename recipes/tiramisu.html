Tiramisu

Picture a decadent symphony of textures. Delicate sponge fingers, softened by a whisper of strong coffee, nestle against a velvety symphony of creamy bliss. A touch of bittersweet dust dances playfully on top, like moonlight on snow. This, my friend, is the essence of tiramisu: a poem of indulgence sung in every bite.

Tiramisu is a classic Italian coffee-flavored dessert. It is made with layers of ladyfingers soaked in espresso, mascarpone cheese filling, and cocoa powder. The ladyfingers are dipped in the espresso mixture and then arranged in a dish. The mascarpone cheese filling is made with eggs, sugar, mascarpone cheese, and heavy cream. It is spread over the ladyfingers, and then the dessert is dusted with cocoa powder.

Ingredients :

  • Egg Yolks
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Cream
  • Vanilla
  • Rum
  • ladyfingers
  • cocoa powder

Steps :

  1. Make the Filling
  2. Soak the Ladyfingers
  3. Assemble the Tiramisu
