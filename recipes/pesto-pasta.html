Pesto Pasta

Imagine emerald waves crashing against shores of al dente spirals. The air whispers of garlic's tang and basil's verdant song. Parmesan rains down a shower of salty kisses, each bite a dance on the tongue. This, my friend, is a taste of Ligurian sunshine, captured in every strand of pesto pasta.

Sun-kissed strands of pasta twirl through a vibrantly green embrace, painted with the whispers of fresh basil. Sun-warmed pine nuts scatter like jewels, promising whispers of toasty richness. This is a summer serenade on a plate, a melody of pesto pasta.

Ingredients :

  • Pasta
  • Onion and oil
  • Pesto
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps :

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
