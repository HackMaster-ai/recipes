Lasagna

Layers of golden promise, whispering comfort in soft whispers of tomato. Rich ribbons of pasta embrace a symphony of savory bliss, each forkful a journey through molten cheese and earthy whispers of meat. This, my friend, is lasagna: a love poem baked in a pan, bursting with warmth in every bite.

Imagine a tapestry woven with ribbons of sun-kissed dough, cradling treasures of simmered sauce and melty cheeses. Each layer bursts with whispers of oregano's caress and basil's verdant dance. A molten kiss of tomato bathes the palate, promising comfort and richness with every bite. This, my friend, is lasagna: a siren song of Italian indulgence, calling your taste buds home.

Ingredients :

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spice and seasonings
  • Lasagna nodles
  • cheeses
  • Egg

Steps :

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
